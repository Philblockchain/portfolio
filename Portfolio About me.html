About me

Welcome to my personal journey where discipline and resilience form the cornerstone of my multifaceted career. With a robust foundation in Sports Rehabilitation, my professional life is rooted in the principles of physical endurance and perpetual growth.

My passion for running mirrors my professional ethos - constantly pushing forward, striving for excellence. As I craft this portfolio, I am enthusiastically expanding my skillset in the digital realm, diving into the intricacies of HTML and CSS. It's a thrilling new chapter that speaks to my love for emerging technologies and my dedication to continuous learning.

My exploration of the digital frontier is paralleled by an immersive three-year journey into the depths of blockchain technology. This expedition has not only deepened my understanding but fueled a profound engagement with its transformative potential.

At BIZI Labs, as a Product Manager, I am at the vanguard of crafting an exceptional Web3 mobile experience. My role transcends the boundaries of product development, encompassing the education of startups in blockchain technology, fostering pivotal partnerships, and devising innovative solutions to industry challenges. Design is yet another realm where I express my creativity and precision. Proficient in Adobe XD and Figma, I specialize in wireframing and prototyping, crafting user interfaces that are as intuitive as they are captivating.

'Getting BIZI with Blockchain' is my latest initiative - a magazine that breaks down the complexities of blockchain for the everyday reader. It's the culmination of my expertise, passion, and commitment to education, offering a platform for community engagement and shared knowledge. Join me on this extraordinary voyage where endurance meets innovation, and every step forward is a stride towards redefining the digital landscape.
